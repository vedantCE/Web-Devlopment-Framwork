CHARUSAT Admission FAQ

What are the eligibility criteria for undergraduate programs at CHARUSAT?
Applicants must have passed 12th (HSC) with the required percentage and relevant subjects, usually including Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology, depending on the course.
How can I apply for admission at CHARUSAT?
You can apply online through the official CHARUSAT admission portal. The application link is generally made available on the university website during the admission period.
Is there an entrance exam for CHARUSAT admission?
Admission to most programs is based on merit (like GUJCET, JEE, or GSEB scores). However, some courses may have their own entrance tests or interviews.
What documents are required during the admission process?
You’ll typically need your 10th and 12th mark sheets, transfer certificate, GUJCET/JEE scorecard, passport-sized photographs, ID proof, and category certificate (if applicable).
What is the admission process for international or NRI students?
CHARUSAT offers NRI and international student quotas. Such applicants need to apply through the International Admissions section with necessary academic equivalency and visa documents.
Is hostel accommodation available for new students?
Yes, CHARUSAT provides on-campus hostel facilities for both boys and girls with dining, security, and recreational amenities.
Can I get admission through management quota?
Yes, limited seats are available under the management quota. Interested students should directly contact the admissions office for eligibility and procedure.
Are there any scholarships available for new students?
Yes, CHARUSAT offers scholarships based on merit, financial need, and performance in entrance exams. Details are available on the official scholarship page.
How can I check my admission status after applying?
Once you've submitted your application, you can log in to the portal using your credentials to track your admission status and updates.
When does the academic session start for new admissions?
Typically, the academic session begins in July or August each year. Exact dates are announced on the official website and via email/SMS notifications to admitted students.
